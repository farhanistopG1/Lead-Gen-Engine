# Lead-Gen-Engine

AI-powered local business prospecting engine that automates lead discovery, website analysis, opportunity detection, and personalized outreach generation.

## Problem

Manual prospecting is slow.

Finding businesses, reviewing websites, identifying problems, and crafting personalized outreach messages can take hours per prospect.

## Solution

Lead-Gen-Engine automates the workflow by:

- Discovering businesses through Google Maps
- Collecting contact information
- Analyzing websites
- Detecting improvement opportunities
- Generating personalized outreach assets
- Managing campaigns through Google Sheets

## Features

- Google Maps lead discovery
- Website scraping and analysis
- AI-powered opportunity detection
- Personalized outreach generation
- Campaign tracking
- Duplicate prevention
- Retry handling
- Logging and monitoring

## Architecture

Campaign
↓
Lead Discovery
↓
Website Analysis
↓
AI Processing
↓
Outreach Generation
↓
Campaign Tracking
